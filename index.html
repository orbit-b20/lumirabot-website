What is LumiraBot?

LumiraBot is a modern platform built to deliver clear, accurate, and easy-to-understand information. The name comes from *Lumen* = light/clarity and *Bot* = helpful resource.

We cut through complexity to give you straightforward content. Now expanded with on-chain presence via our token $LUMI, launched on the Base network through Bankr.
